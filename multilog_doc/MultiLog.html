MessageTypes
mt (Message Type) and lt (Log Type) prefixes are used elsewhere but mt is worse because there's already mtWarning and mtInformation.
The existing lt do not makes confusion.
MessageTypes form a distribution of function's type groups: HOW \ with WHICH method's group to log.
